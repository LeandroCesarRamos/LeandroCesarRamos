About my HTML5 skill

When I started in this field, I didn’t have much knowledge about how to create or manage a website. I knew that each site had a specific purpose, but I never imagined how it actually worked behind the scenes. My curiosity led me to research and learn about the entire creation process. Today, I use this skill daily in almost everything I build.

With HTML5, I create responsive websites and take advantage of its new features, such as embedding audio and video without external plugins. I also frequently use geolocation tools, which are essential for projects that require location-based functionality.

In addition, I work with:
• Semantic elements to improve accessibility and SEO.
• Forms with advanced validation and input types.
• Integration of APIs for dynamic and interactive content.
• Canvas and SVG for graphics and animations.
• Local storage and session storage for client-side data management.
• Progressive enhancement to ensure compatibility across browsers and devices.

HTML5 is the foundation of my web development projects, allowing me to deliver modern, efficient, and user-friendly experiences.
